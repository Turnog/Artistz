Apple Watch Series 9

Apple Watch Series 9 features a custom-designed Apple silicon chip in the all-new S9 SiP. Apple's most powerful watch chip delivers system-wide improvements and exciting new features, including new double-tap gestures and on-device Siri integration, along with the ability to access and record health data privately and securely. Apple Watch Series 9 also features a new 4-core Neural Engine that can process machine learning tasks up to twice as fast as Apple Watch Series 8. The power efficiency of the S9 SiP helps Apple Watch Series 9 maintain all-day battery life of up to 18 hours.
